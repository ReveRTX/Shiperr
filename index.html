Who we are?

At Shiperr, we are a dynamic logistics aggregator committed to simplifying delivery for businesses and individuals. Our platform connects customers with reliable delivery partners, offering fast, safe, and efficient parcel transportation. Founded by Akula Mahendra and co-founded by S B Harsha Vardhan, Shiperr was created to revolutionize logistics with innovative technology and customer-centric solutions. With a Zero Commission model and minimal daily subscription fees for drivers, YOU GET WHAT YOU SEE! Customers can book with our Pay Later option and enjoy cashback on every booking. We offer real-time tracking, flexible options, and a mission to empower businesses with cost-effective, hassle-free deliveries.
